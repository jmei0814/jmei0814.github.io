Hi, nice to meet you!

I entered UIUC as an Information Sciences major and graduated in May 2025 with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering with highest honors. I'm now a returning master's student specializing in computer hardware at the Grainger College of Engineering.

I've grown up with robotics — from First Lego League in elementary school to my current presidency of iRobotics, the largest robotics RSO on campus. I try to involve myself with practical robotics development as much as possible.

When I'm not being productive, working on passion projects, or drowning in schoolwork, I enjoy playing video games, performing and composing music, and being bad at volleyball.
